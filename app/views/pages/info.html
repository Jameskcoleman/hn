The Humanist Literary Networks project is an ongoing project that was launched in September 2013.

This network visualization was initially created on 11/24/14. Most recent update: 11/24/14.

Comments and questions can be directed to james dot k dot coleman at gmail dot com.


The sources of the data presented here are the following early modern editions of collected letters:

  • Aretino, Pietro. Lettere di M. Pietro Aretino. Vol. 1. Paris, 1609.
  • Bembo, Pietro. Delle lettere di M. Pietro Bembo. Vol. 3. Venice, 1552.
  • Manuzio, Paolo. Lettere volgari di M. Paolo Manuzio, divise in quattro libri. Venice, 1560.
